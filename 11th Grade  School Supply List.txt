Since high school schedules can vary quite a bit with different math levels, languages, and electives, our grade-level lists are based on the standard class track. If your student’s schedule looks a little different this year, just take a quick peek at our Supplies by Class lists to make sure they're fully covered!
11th & 12th Grades 
(Note: Juniors and Seniors have more electives; those supplies may not be in the list below. Please touch base with us at orientation to solidify what you will need for dual credit courses or electives.)
Folders:
* 2 Blue plastic with prongs (Memory Work)
* 2 White plastic with prongs (Research)
* 1 folder with brads (Leadership)
* 1 regular folder (Bible)
* Folders for chosen electives
Notebooks & Paper:
* 1 Composition or spiral notebook (Leadership)
* 3 Spiral notebooks (Literature, 2 for Bible)
* Loose-leaf paper
* Notebooks for chosen electives
Writing & Organization:
* A planner is suggested
* Plenty of pens and pencils
Books (11th Grade):
* Personal Bible
* The British Tradition I: Poetry, Prose, & Drama
* Frankenstein
Technology:
* A typing device (laptop or iPad with keyboard)
Other Items to note:
* Dress Code
* Decorate your lockers with magnetic items, but please avoid using stickers or anything that requires tape.
* Please have extra pencils and notebook paper. Also, be sure to keep a stash of these at home, too.
